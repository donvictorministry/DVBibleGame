Why I Created This Game
A Cognitive Sanctuary
for the Body of Christ

Welcome, beloved. You might wonder what a memory game has to do with the gospel. This was not built as a mere distraction but as a cognitive sanctuary for the Body of Christ. In our rapidly moving digital world, our minds are under constant attack from noise, stress, and worldly distractions. Taking a brief and wholesome pause to focus your mind helps lower daily anxiety, center your thoughts, and bring your peace back to the Kingdom.

Therefore, since we are surrounded by such a great cloud of witnesses, let us throw off everything that hinders and the sin that so easily entangles. And let us run with perseverance the race marked out for us.

— Hebrews 12:1 KJV

By actively engaging your memory with the names of our biblical heroes and ultimately the powerful name of our LORD JESUS CHRIST, we are reminded of this very cloud of witnesses. Training your working memory directly enhances your everyday ability to retain Scripture, recall biblical history, and stay sharp when hearing and sharing the Gospel with others.

How to Play

You have exactly 2 minutes (120 seconds) to find all matching pairs. Tap the gold cards to reveal the silver names beneath. Match every biblical hero before the countdown ends. The board reshuffles completely every single time you play.

Rewards & Spiritual Ranks

Match all cards before time expires and earn a spiritual rank based on your speed:

  • Abundant time remaining — Master Scholar Crown
  • Moderate time remaining — Faithful Warrior Shield
  • Finish just in time — Dedicated Reader Scroll

Train your mind. Remember the Word. Win souls.
